Ahoj,

  PythonHandler neni nic jineho nez to co bys dal v interaktivnim pythonu do
"import", tj. nejdriv si zkontroluj jestli mas v site-packages
mod-python/publisher.py.

  Dalsi vec:

<Location /mpinfo>
  SetHandler mod_python
  PythonHandler mod_python.testhandler
</Location>
 ... a pak restartuj Apache a navstiv http://<mujweb>/mpinfo  - tim si
overis funkcnost celeho mod_pythonu
