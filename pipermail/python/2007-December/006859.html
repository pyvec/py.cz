----
<form action="./vypis?kkkk=uuuu" method="post">

</form>
----

{'kkkk': [Field('kkkk', 'uuuu')], 'nick': [Field('nick', 'skuska')],

metodou poslane...

a osobitne nejake pole samych POST premennych(ano, hladam nieco take,
