ahoj vespolek. nasi polsti kamaradi organizuji mini konferenci ve varsave
uz za par dni (25-26 kveten). i kdyz last-minute, pozadali me o preposlani
informaci - urcite to bude skvela akce, tak snad jeste nekoho oslovi:

Hi folks!

I would like to invite all of you to the Python Conference held in Warsaw
on 25-26th of May!

Here's some more detailed information:

PyWaw Summit is a conference organized for everyone interested in
Python-related technology, taking place in Warsaw, Poland on 25-26th of
May, 2015. Two days of great talks in English led by mix of local and
international speakers. The aim is to provide valuable content in a
friendly atmosphere that favours experience sharing and fun.

The conference derives from experienced local Python community called
PyWaw, which actively organizes monthly meetups for almost 4 years. There
were already more than 45 meetings and over 100 presentations. Willing our
community to grow, PyWaw decided to go one step beyond and organize a
conference that makes a dent in the Python universe.

What’s more we have special 10% discount for you. Please enter the
"pywaw4czech" into notes field in registration form to apply the promocode.
In case of any questions please contact us at info na pywaw.org.

Find us on:

Web: http://summit.pywaw.org/­
Twitter: @PyWawSummit
Facebook: /PyWawSummit­

All the best,
PyWaw Summit Team
