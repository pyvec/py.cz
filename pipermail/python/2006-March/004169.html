metoda Add muze mit i dalsi parametry
(http://wxpython.org/docs/api/wx.GridBagSizer-class.html#Add), Vam by
se hodil asi border (pocet pixelu) v kombinaci s flag:

  sizer.Add(item, pos, border=5, flag=wx.LEFT | wx.RIGHT)

tento zpusob se pouziva i u jinych "sizeru" -
http://wxpython.org/docs/api/wx.Sizer-class.html#Add
